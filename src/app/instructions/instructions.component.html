Abandon your principles, exit your tortutred soul role for 15 minutes, and write something for a change.
Come on, Camus, chop-chop.

The Rulebook

For the next 15 minutes, your only mission will be to finish a 300-word short story.

  • When you start, a writing box will appear and the timer will start counting down. You can’t pause the timer.
  • We’ll give you the first sentence to light the spark & you’ll take it from there.
  • A few more sentences will appear down the road to force a plot twist on you. Maybe.
  • You can’t delete the props; their job is to make you think fast—don’t overthink it.
  • The timer will warn you near the end to avoid cutting you off mid-thought.

Follow the button to, well, start writing. Have fun, you!
